Art Resources

YouTube is my go-to when I want to practice a specific skill or get inspired. I collect my favorite videos in a watercolor video playlist. I'm also a fan of Marc Taro Holmes, and his book "The Urban Sketcher". One of my favorite essays on painting is by Winston Churchill, "Painting as a Pasttime". Who knew he loved to paint in his free time? He was quite good at it. Over time I've amassed equipment faster than skill, and am currently using a 12 or 24 color Koi Field Sketch kit with a Pentel or Yasutomo Niji water brush. For
