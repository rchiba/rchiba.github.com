I finally got up and decided to ink and vector-ize my sketches. Now their ready for viewing on DeviantArt, so check that out if you're bored out of your mind. I mean, that seems to be the only reason anybody every comes here or anywhere affiliated with me. I don't have the pizzaz to attract people, both in real life and on the Internet. Isn't it silly?

You know what else is silly? The grass really is always greener on the other side.

Fertilizer is so overrated. Why are people so obsessed over the color of their lawn? I don't see how brown is so much worse than green. It's like our neighboors are trying to over-compensate for their gas guzzling by making their lawns greener. Little do they know that fertilizer is made out of oil too.

Bah! Green lawns are for people who care about grass. As for me, I don't care what lawns look like.
