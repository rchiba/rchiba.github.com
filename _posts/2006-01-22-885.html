Usually, I turn my stereo on and that's how I listen to music when I'm in my room. This is because the stereo has three large speakers that surround my room, compared to two built-in speakers for my laptop, and two weak speakers for my alarm-clock radio. Today, I decided to change it up a bit by playing my music through the speakers on my laptop. Through this change, I have discovered something very facinating. The speakers for my laptop are located beneath the plastic that I rest my wrists on while I type. The neat thing about this is that the music vibrates through the plastic so I can "feel" the music. Although the bass is weak, its a brand new experience. Something new, I guess. I'm not sure if it's worth mentioning, but who cares?
