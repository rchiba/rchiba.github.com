Anyways, the inevitability of something going terribly wrong while the day is going on was putting pressure on me. That's when I started listening to jazz. It really calms you down. I was doing my homework and I felt like exploding. I listened to the jazz and cooled down. It's probably a stage. Yes... a stage....

Anyways, at lunch, the people who sit with me (Joe, Eric, and this short kid) always leave me. It's so depressing. I have no where to sit except by myself. Crazy kid I tell ya... But there are other things... worse things than sitting alone by yourself, with no one to talk to in a crowded lunch room. Things like... forgetting my homework as such.

I have so much on my mind. I have so much.
