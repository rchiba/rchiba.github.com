Today is going to be one of the last clear days of the season. Outside is pristine blue, yellow, red, and orange. The last, nice fall day. And I'm sitting here in front of my computer typing this up. Sad.

Now, let's talk about school. I sit at a table of 4. All the "cool" nerds sit at this other table. This "other" table is always packed, sometimes people are even standing around this table. I have yet to be able to infiltrate this table. My purpose is to sit with my friends and chat. My current table involves little discussion or even semi-logical talk. The usual conversations at this table usually involve vulgar descriptions of random people, insults, and talk about exploding things. It really disturbs me. I think Joe has veered off course a bit. I wish he would stop talking about gliders and boats. If that's his passion, that's great, but instead of talking to me about it, he should go build one. I mean, he also wants to be a game designer. He doesn't even know what goes into making a game. You can't just pour out ideas and get paid for it. It takes hard work. He should understand that before going and charging into unknown territory. I still want to be friends with him, but it still bothers me sometimes.
