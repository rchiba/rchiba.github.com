I have willed myself to wake up an hour early to update my blog. I believe that I try to contradict myself subconciously. Maybe it's because I'm a teenager and teenagers are supposed to be angsty and contradictory. Obviously, all teenagers are angsty and contradictory, just like how all African Americans are basketball stars, and just like how all asians are nerds, and just like all...

Anyways, I woke up groggily this morning, staring at my Pikachu clock. I usually wake up like this. I wake up 30 minutes before I am "supposed" to wake up and periodically stare at my clock. Stare at my clock and wish... plead with my delusioned mind to get more sleep. That's the state I'm in right now. I'm half delusioned.

What is my other half then? If I was just delusioned, why would I even be writing this post? My other side is in a state of fear and helplessness. I am going to go to diving practice today and I am going to have to try the.... "double". This double is dangerous for me because I know that I will smack. It's fated that I will experience extreme pain. That's the problem. As I sit here listening to a Ray Charles CD that my mother got at Target, I feel like a helpless animal. A helpless animal trapped in a cage, to eventually be experimented on and killed. The pain is imminent, and my heart is racing. What am I to do?

Well, I could go to diving and suffer my fate... I'll probably do that. Shoot, I just talked myself into going...

Anyways, Joe said that I'm "rubbing off" on him after he posted something negative. I never knew that I was that negative. Come on, I'm normal... right?
