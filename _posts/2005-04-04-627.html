My English teacher was introducing our "Totalitarianism Paper" to us. This paper had a selection of dictators we could choose from, including the infamous Kim Jong Il. Because of the school corporation's budget printers, the "Il" in "Kim Jong Il" looked like "II", or a Roman 2. Because of this, the teacher called Kim Jong Il "Kim Jong the Second." I just thought that was the most hilarious thing I've heard in the past month. I suppose English teachers only teach English, but you should at least know who KIM JONG IL is!

Anyways, my group consists of Kaiser Andrew, myself, and Russell. Russell didn't want to pick Kim Jong Il (like I wanted too) because it would stir up too much controversey on the Internet. He said that there would be many blogs that would pose too much of a hassle to deal with when we searched the Internet. Absurd. I still think that Kim Jong Il was the better choice.
