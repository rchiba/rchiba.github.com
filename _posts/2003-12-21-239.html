I feel like he's crushing my spirit slowly, or in Ryo's terms, stabbing paper clips into my eyes. Mom wont let me see Sunny right now, I might not see her tomorrow and its been about two, three weeks and I'm near my wit's end. I really need someone to talk to. I'm just too tired to care.
