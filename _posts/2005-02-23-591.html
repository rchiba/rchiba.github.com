The dismemberment of diving was supposed to bring me joy. Now all I focus is on homework. As I focus on this homework, it always seems like everyone is always faster, smarter, more efficient, just plain better than me at doing everything. I don't raise my hand anymore. Perhaps I have become paranoid of making a fool out of myself. I hate myself, because everyone else is better than me. Every waking moment of my school life has been transformed into a self-hate filled hell. Constantly, I am clumsy. Constantly, I am mentally truant. Constantly, I am a failure. I am obsolete. I hate myself so much.

Sometimes it is easier to be angry and frustrated than happy. It's just a matter of convenience. You need strong will power to overcome your own criticism. I'm not strong enough. That's why I'm on a lower tier than you. That is why I am a worthless bag of nothingness and you are.... a worthless bag of something.

I make myself sick. I am disgusting. My disgustingly low test grades and my disgustingly slow math skills make me want to implode within myself. I am constantly perpetuating self hate now. I can't help it. It's such a "vicious" cycle. Grr...

Anyways, I'm planning to sell batteries at school. I'm not sure if it's legal, but the outrageous priced school batteries justifies my capitalistic crusade. The end justify the means...

I'm not so happy right now.
