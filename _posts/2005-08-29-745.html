Everyone should become a robot and live in a fabricated world with nothing to motivate them, but nothing to neagate their tasks, not like these robots had minds anyways. Or not.

Back to the instruments thing. Well, what I'm trying to do is justify the fact that I did not pick up my clarinet and practice my music, like I was "supposed" to do for today. It's pretty sad that I try to justify my procrastination by writing about it. I mean, most people just do it. They have guts. I have to go out and complain and whine about it to myself to get myself to feel better. It's absolutely disgusting.

About the instruments. People try to make music, but why make music when you can just let others make it for you. It's not like I'm going to make a living from playing my clarinet. It's not like I derive any pleasure from playing it either. Then, you're probably wondering why even continue band. Well, I'm just trying to justify the purchase of my expensive clarinet. The thing is, I just can't let something so expensive go so easily. I hate playing, I hate being worse than everyone else, I hate practicing, I would hate to let the money go to waste, so I trudge along, trying to justify the existance of my clarinet.

My clarinet rules my world. It's not fun.
