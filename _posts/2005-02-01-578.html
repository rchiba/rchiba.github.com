Wait, no I don't. I had such a great witty parody of a common saying that I wanted to present, but unfortunately, my memory is fading. I assume it is because I don't want to remember what happened today. I am protecting myself by erasing my own memory. I don't think I erased my memory for something very traumatic. I think I erased my own memory because I find the things I supposedly "learn" in some of my classes pointless. The "Orientation to Live and Careers" class is a prime example. I "learned" how to read "better". All she did was point out that you should actually read the things that you study for. Give me a break. No duh, you have to actually read the things that you want to learn. What, now the state of Indiana has to tell me how to "study"!? How absurd!

Anyways, I am being harsh at the OLC teacher and the state of Indiana, since there are some "less talented" people who don't know how to study. I have no idea how they even get through life without learning and studying, but that's beside the point.

The point is that OLC is still on the "I HATE THIS CLASS" list. The only thing that it is teaching me is to be witty. I get so bored in that reject class that I get witty on the worksheets and get witty with my peers to amuse myself. It's like if you locked me in a madhouse with a strict Nazi loyalist. How absurd!

Tomorow I am going to bring disco music to diving. How absurd!

I'm absurd!
