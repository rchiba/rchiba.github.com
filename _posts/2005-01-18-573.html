Anyways, think about how many ways being spontaneous would come in handy. Being creative like this could result in new ways of thinking of solutions to problems. Creative problem solving comes in handy in many jobs. As long as speaking more creativity, my Taco Bell Fiesta Bowl and Mountain Dew has caused an increase in my speed of typing. Don't ask me where that came from, because I'm still working on my spontaneous creativity skills. I need to get off of this caffine high and go back to my dull state of normality.
