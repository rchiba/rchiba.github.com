Jazz means it's past 9pm. Past 9pm means that I better hurry and sleep.

So, the robotics season has started. So will school. It's an overwhelming but justified venture, I think. Working as a team is hard. I feel socially retarded sometimes. With me, talking gets awkward fast.

That's life. It's not Walgreens, that's for sure. But hey, with nothing else to complain about, I'm in good shape.

This feeling. Being busy enough to not be able to stop and think about it all. Bad, good? Like I said, I can't even let myself stop and think about how bad or good it is. Just, let it happen.
