This means that I have less than half an hour to post, my deadline.

Right now, I don't know how I'm awake. In the past few days, I haven't slept. And so my sleep debt grows larger and larger. I really can't figure out how I'm still moving right now. One would figure that something as essential as sleep is... well, essential.

Will I challenge a fourth day? A fifth? My lifetime?

But, it seems like I have no choice. So the answer would be the last one. I have to say, my purist work ethic will be the end of me.

Now, there's a speech to be written, a book to be finished, a worksheet to be written upon, a test to be studied for, and an animation to render.

But, am I getting anything done if I can't feel emotions anymore? What is reality but what one feels? My mind is dull with time. My hands shake even more than they usually do. I can't focus my eyes on one spot and do things efficiently anymore.

So, my life's a cycle of doom. I have to get things done, so I stay up. Staying up makes me get things done at a slower pace, so I have to stay up even longer. So on and so forth. Blah. Not good.

Or is it?

I can't even tell anymore
