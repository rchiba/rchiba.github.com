On a positive note, I'll be building bridges tomorrow at Tiffany's house. I found some Gorilla Glue at my house and used it to make vermicelli triangles. Who knew that something you see in SkyMall magazine would be so useful?

Walking into the Barber Shop, I felt anxious on the inside. I put on a smile and tried not to show what was going through my mind. This is a half a years worth of hair man, what are you doing? These old, but lively barbers have a tendency to take as much as they can off of my head. I've known from three years of their service. Half an hour and two chapters of Moby Dick later, I'm gazing into a mirror looking at my hair. It's different.

It's a bad thing to be a vain person, but wouldn't you feel like staring into a mirror after taking off what feels like whole kilograms of hair?
