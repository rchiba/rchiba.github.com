But, I'll wait until I'm desperate. I need to save up my happiness energy. Right now, I'm in that "I don't care, blah, blah, blah" attitude that appears to invade the life of everyone nowadays. Just floating along, not really anchored to a goal or a task or a mission.

I felt jealous today. I felt dark today. I felt worried. But, I also felt lucky. And I felt satisfied.

It's just a mix, you know? And you can really pull anything from it, besides the idea that all of this really doesn't matter. Next week, it'll be the same deal.
