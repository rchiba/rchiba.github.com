That's what makes it typical right?

Endless minutes wasted loathing over products on thinkgeek.com have made me unhappy. My unhappyness is also stimulated by my depressing music of radioheadness...

In French, I've decided I'm an utter failure. I'm just not a French person. Languages don't come easy for me. As I make these excuses up, I realize that doing this is just a sad way of trying to compensate for my failures. I try to justify my failure. That makes me sick.

I was daydreaming in class today and I thought about getting a handgun. You know, in the movies the guy with the gun always wins. If there's an apockalypse, you've got a (temporary) safeguard. A handgun would be a nice commodity. As I daydreamed, I recognized my misjudgement. If I had a handgun, I would probably hurt myself in situations like this. Note to self: don't buy weapons.

I guess that's the lesson of the day. I've just got to stick to my pacifistic morals and everybody will benefit.
