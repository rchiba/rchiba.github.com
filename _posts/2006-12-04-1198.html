--- layout: post title: '' tags: - Blast From the Past status: private type: post published: false meta: blogger_blog: ryochiba.blogspot.com blogger_author: Ryohttp://www.blogger.com/profile/08925049298023370483noreply@blogger.com blogger_permalink: /2006/12/walking-through-frigid-air-to-my-van-on.html --- Walking through the frigid air to my van, on the sun-splashed black asphalt, I realized what we mean by "Happy Birthday." My door opens with a dull clunk, I start the engine, look out the window and wave to Adam.

The message we send is that every day is special. A moment of life should never be taken for granted, and our creation of the "everyday birthday" is a vehicle for this message. We live for only so long. So, take those risky chances, work to your white-knuckle limits, treat others with exceptional care. Simply, live your life like it's your birthday.

Happy Birthday.
