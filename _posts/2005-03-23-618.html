--- layout: post title: '' tags: - Blast From the Past status: private type: post published: false meta: blogger_blog: ryochiba.blogspot.com blogger_author: Ryohttp://www.blogger.com/profile/08925049298023370483noreply@blogger.com blogger_permalink: /2005/03/you-wouldnt-believe-what-influence.html --- You wouldn't believe what an influence fashion has on high school society. It's a bit too integral, if you ask me.

I walked into school today with a collared overshirt and khakis and everyone asks if the world is ending. You see, I usually wear 2 layers of fleece. This drastic change caused many others who know me to stare, exclaim, and generally panic. The major unanimous consensus was that this change was for the better. Although I don't see myself as a vain person, I enjoyed all of the attention I got. It's only human nature, you know?

Anyways, in this fashion oriented society, vanity should be degraded. In a cultured social class like mine, the thing that really matters is how witty you are. If you're straight, to the point, and not sarcastically comical you'll probably get the boot. Wit can take you a long way. Nice threads can make you look good while getting there.

Well, the question is... is vanity all what it's cracked up to be? I mean, should we be more focused on what someone wears than how witty they are or how rational they are? Is some fab rock star going to manipulate us more than some disfigured genius? Most likely. Judging by appearances and all...
