Unbelievable, but there he was. For six hours he had toiled away in bed. The hot embers of illness burned a patch on his head, and he could no longer see. Breathing was a chore, and the air was either too hot or too cold. Hopeless, tired, the sweat formed beads on his skin, yet the bed felt chilly. Wandering around in his head was the notion that there would come a time when all of these pains and aches would go away. But, the day was hard still. The vague understanding never came to be, and again he was forced to suffer through the whole ordeal. Slowly, as civilizations crumble under the weight of time, darkness came. The nightmares were to continue.
