Lots of work, and homework, and more work, and some more work on top of that. It really is enough work to shake your red, commie fist at.

Right now, I really can't think of anything to say.

Besides being ripped off by the Motorola customer service reps, for the fifth time, I can't think of anything particularly outstanding today.

Wait, actually there is something that stood out today.

At DDR club today, there were 3 people who told me that I looked very sad. I know that at the time, my body was in a critical low-blood-sugar crisis. To curve my intense hunger and dizzyness, I bumbled my way to the nearest vending machine and bought some toaster pastries. I ate them, and got a headache. Too much sugar never helps. The artificial taste of the starchy "bread" part of the pastry did not help my condition.

And so, while in this state of calamity, I apparently looked sad.

The "lesson" learned? (This lesson is the lesson that I usually make up to justify living for one more day)

Hungry people look sad.
