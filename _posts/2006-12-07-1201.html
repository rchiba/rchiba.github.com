But, it's so easy to procrastinate. So, very difficult to break out of this vegetativeness.

You know what? I like it when I don't have anything to write here. Because it means that there isn't any drama in my life. Short paragraphs, meaningless posts, it means that I'm living life like I want to and that I'm feeling fine. It means I have nothing to get angry about, nothing to get sad about. And who wants to write when you're happy? You start to realize all those things you could have been doing while moping around.

I think Mr. Chen, my guidance counselor, still thinks I'm suicidal. English teacher must have sent down some more of my essays or something. He looked genuinely worried.

Too bad I'm not sad. I guess Mr. Chen won't have anything to find in me!
