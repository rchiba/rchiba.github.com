DEET is that stuff that smells like sweet pine trees, if anyone decided to make pine-tree flavoured candy. I don't know how someone would sell such a thing, but let's move on.

DEET is a chemical named, "N-diethyl-meta-toluamide" and it happens to prevent mosquito bites. It works well against ticks too. It looks something like this:





It was made by the Army after they suffered the effects of insect-transmitted disease in the jungle warfare of WWII. DEET entered combat service in 1946 and just 10 years later it was released to the public.

A note of caution: Don't spray DEET on spandex. Bad things will happen.
