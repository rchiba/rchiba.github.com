Box
Originally uploaded by Aminalz Rule.
I fashioned a crude case for my camera out of a small cardboard box. There's a hole for the shutter and lens, and it does its job of hiding my camera. I took this picture in the bathroom mirror at school during lunch. It's no ordinary box, my friend. It's a box with an eye.
