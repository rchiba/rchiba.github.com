I looked at my watch and just realized that it changed to a new day. It's really weird to get no sleep, because one just continues one giant day with the same thing happening twice. Currently, I see white blotches in my eye every once in awhile, and I can't type right very well. I also can't do thinking and whatnot. I think the longest recorded time of not sleeping was 11 days, with no drugs. How difficult. So, one night of lost sleep doesn't hurt, right?

The window in my room is a giant sea of black with lots of pretty dots and sometimes I feel like I'm falling even though I'm standing up. And words, I forget what things are called. I stared at my pencil for a few minutes because I forgot what it was called.

Humans are versatile, right? So what's a nights really worth in the 21st century?

I'm droozy.
