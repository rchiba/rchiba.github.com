Why? I suppose all of these problems either stemie from the fact that I hate my Geometry homework and don't understand it, or because I hate the diving practice that I will have to go to in an hour. It's probably a combination. I hate everything in the world. I feel like torturing innocent woodland creatures, I feel like inflicting damage, pain, and wrath. I want to burn and pillage and destroy.
