It is very stormy right now. Many floods abound. Creek flooded. At least one man dead. I hear many thunders. Flash flood warning. DANGER! DANGER! I hope the power doesnt go out. I hope the creek doesnt flood the bridge. My house is on high ground so I am not in danger of flood. The bridge to civilization and many other roads are flooded though. I hope the people living in the flooded houses are safe. I really hope...
