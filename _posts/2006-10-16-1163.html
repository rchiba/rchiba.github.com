That today was a day worth forgetting, that there was nothing of note in these past 20-some hours.

And to tell you the truth, I can't think of anything that made me feel. I was entirely content.

Although, I snapped this afternoon and started to type grouchy and frustrating words to my friends. That was right before I ate dinner, and I was hungry. After eating, I stopped typing angry things and got to work on my various affairs. Go figure.

When I'm about to die, I'll look back to days like this and feel stupid and remorseful for taking this grand life for granted.
