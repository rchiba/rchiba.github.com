After five and a half hours of arduous labor, I have finished my homework. Of course, Ryan and his friends are out watching a movie and having diner. Fun. Fun. Unfortunately, my life is devoid of such social activities. I suppose I am the one denying myself of these assets, but I can't help it. I am not a sociable person. I type here at my computer, do homework, and fix computers. How could I be a sociable person? I can present a speech, yet I can't talk to the person next to me on the bus. I pity myself.

I plan to get Sim City 4 someday and play it. Unfortunately, not now. Maybe a year from now, when I have more time. Most likely, I won't have more time a year from now. I suppose this is supposed to be my "golden years". For some reason these years of constant terror, disgust, and absolute distaste for myself and those around me. I am full of stress, overworked, and tired. But I am one of the lucky ones. I am lucky...

Why do I live in a society that scorns self-excellence in knowledge, and encourages the playing of childish games, the wasting of resources, and a bi-lateral system of government? Is this really America? The land of the free? Home of the brave? Is bravery so important, as to sacrifice the Earth's natural resources? Who am I to say...
