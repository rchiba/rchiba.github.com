Oddly, it's a positive response.

Am I sadistic in being positive when I read about my peer's negativity? Do I take some sick pleasure in reading the details of others' woes?

It bothers me that reading over everyone else's misfortune makes me feel better, but I can't seem to change how I react. My sky feels less cloudy when the grass isn't greener on the other side.

Don't tell anybody.
