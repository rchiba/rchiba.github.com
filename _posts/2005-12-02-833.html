I have a terrible cold.

Thus, I took some medicine, and now I'm in a drug-induced stupor.

This sort of feeling is the kind of feeling that makes everything around you seem like a dream. I can't feel my face. When I run my fingers through my hair, the hair doesn't feel like it's mine. I wash my hands, but it feels like there's no water, and I'm just going through the motions on instinct. And when I walk, I float.

My homework is to read a section in a book I have. I don't think I'll be able to do it because the words on the page don't make any sense to me right now.

I don't see how I'll survive tomorrow's QuizBowl tournament.

Say farewell to this screwed guy.
