The reason is because I want to go back to my old house in Minnesota, back to my old friends. I had a real life there, but here, I do nothing to get to know my environment and surroundings. I've tied to forget about my grief but it will never leave my mind, not until I make my grief happy by going back but that will undoubtedly never happen.
