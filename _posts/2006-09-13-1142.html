I remember crying the first time I saw it. It was a heart-wrenching movie at the time of my viewing. It came out in 1995, and I remember it being new-ish when I saw it. Now, I have this odd sort of connection to the movie. I watch it and everything is the same. The plot, the character. Each frame is a landmark from my past.

Like so many other landmarks from my past.
