--- layout: post title: '' tags: - Blast From the Past status: private type: post published: false meta: blogger_blog: ryochiba.blogspot.com blogger_author: Ryohttp://www.blogger.com/profile/08925049298023370483noreply@blogger.com blogger_permalink: /2006/11/behind-counter-in-tidy-arrangement.html --- Behind the counter, in a tidy arrangement, they sit. Something that has been five years in the making. Twelve of them, neatly stacked in the corner. Boxes of white printed with grey, sans-serif text, the Mac-esque marketing campaign that just oozes with hipness. The man in the store outfit leans over and puts one of them in a plastic bag. A crowd of about twenty pasty college students mingle under cardboard cutouts of green-shirted elven swordsmen, exploding cars, and British spies.

The plastic bag reaches the top of the counter. A credit card is scanned, blip. Someone starts counting down, and the rest join. "Ten seconds before midnight!", someone yells.

We stand behind the counter, away from the crowd, staring forlornly at the pile of modular treasures. If only, if only...

"THREE, TWO, ONE, WIIIIIIII!!!"

And with that said, a new era begins. Transactions flood the cashier, and the group of coveted game consoles escape in the arms of eager fanboys and fangirls.

We stand at the door, spending the remainder of our time wishing that we could be them, and they could be us.
