Now that I know people are reading this, the writing has become a lot less personal. That pisses me right off. Nowadays, feel the urge to hide what I say, even at home, staring at this anonymous blank text box. What if I say something that is misinterpretted? What if someone gets angry at me for saying this-or-that? Life isn't all optimistic and full of dainty little elves throwing chocolates. It's full of some bad things. Of course, I reveal them here and then my act's exposed, eh?

When so many people know who I pretend to be, who I am, who I'm not, I just feel so shallow. Deep people are modest, they don't go and flaunt their emotions online. It's sickening. It's absurd. And here I am. I must be the most shallow person on Earth. Always trying to fish for compliments, trying to boost my ego, and finding nothing real. It's like this twisted, selfish, idiotic passion of mine or something.

You know?
