Frequently asked questions

Can I trial your product before buying?

Yes, we do offer a $7, 7-day, 3-brief trial. Just go to this link to get started.

We also offer a first-month satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our product during your first month, we are happy to give you a full refund, no questions asked.

Can I adjust my plan as I go?

Yes. If you need to scale your plan up or down, just send us a chat in the app, or email support@usetopic.com and we'll be happy to adjust your plan to fit your needs.

When do the briefs reset / do briefs roll over?

Your content briefs reset at the end of the calendar month, regardless of when you start your subscription. Briefs do not roll over, so be sure to use them before the end of the month.

Do we have to pay extra for more people?

Nope. We don't charge you based on how many users you add. 5 users or 50, the price is the same. So invite your entire team!

What languages does Topic support?

Topic officially supports English, French, German, Portuguese, and Italian. Content briefs can target searches from any location in the world in these languages. Need us to support a different language? Email support@usetopic.com and let us know!

Do you have annual billing options?

Yes. We offer either monthly or yearly billing options. The yearly billing option is always cheaper. Reach out to us if you prefer to be billed annually.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancel anytime! Your satisfaction is  important to us, so if at anytime you'd like to cancel you can do so within our platform.
