Frequently asked questions

How much money can I make?

As much as you are capable of. We have no caps!

Our introductory plans start at $50/mo, so let's use that as an example to show you how much you could make:

1 referral = $10 per month
5 referrals = $50 per month
10 referrals = $100 per month
25 referrals = $250 per month
100 referrals = $1000 per month
250 referrals = $2500 per month
1,000 referrals = $10,000 per month

Now imagine that these users upgrade at any point. You could see these numbers double or even triple. Because we pay lifetime commissions, the longer they stay with us, better it is for you. The best customers are those with consistent and ongoing content production needs.

Are there any restrictions on how I can market?

Our most successful affiliates are those making authentic recommendations to an audience that knows them.¬†

If you are running paid search campaigns, the only targeting restriction is you are not allowed to bid on branded keyword search terms such as "UseTopic" and "Topic Pricing".

Do not impersonate Topic or try and trick people into thinking your company is Topic. If you do, you will receive a cease and desist letter and be banned from the partner program.

How long is your cookie window?

The cookie window is 60 days. If a user purchases the product after 60 days from the first referred visit, the conversion won‚Äôt be tracked anymore.

When will my commission be paid?

Commissions are Net-30, paid at the end of every month via PayPal. The minimum payout is $50.

How do you track conversions?

Conversions are tracked via an affiliate link. Coupons are provided to affiliates to encourage their community to participate, but use of the coupon is NOT what is used to track the conversion. Only users who use the provided link will be tracked.

Can I sign up using my own referral link?

No. Unfortunately, self-referrals are strictly prohibited. The aim of a referral program is to encourage affiliates to spread the word about our product(s), not to get discounts.
