Frequently Asked Questions

Can I adjust my plan as I go?
Yes. If you need to scale your plan up or down, you can do so within the app at any time.
What is a user seat? How can I use Topic with an in-house team or outsourced writers?
A user seat is a user who can create and manage content briefs is typically reserved for the editor or content marketing manager. Writers are not counted against the number of user seats on the plan and can collaborate on briefs using a sharable link.
What is a content brief? How many do I need?
A content brief consists of a content research section that helps in creating an outline and a content grader that helps optimize content before publishing. You should plan on using one brief per new article you produce or existing article you optimize.
When do the briefs reset / do briefs roll over?
Your content brief credits reset at the end of the calendar month, regardless of when you start your subscription. Unused briefs for that month roll over into the next, but will expire at the end of the next month.
What languages does Topic support?
Topic officially supports English, French, German, Portuguese, and Italian. Content briefs can target searches from any location in the world in these languages. Need us to support a different language? Email support@usetopic.com and let us know!
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancel anytime! Your satisfaction is  important to us, so if at anytime you'd like to cancel you can do so within our platform.
